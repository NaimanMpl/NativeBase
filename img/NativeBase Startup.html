Frequently Asked Questions

How is NativeBase Startup+ different from NativeBase?

NativeBase Startup+ is built on top of the popular NativeBase component library. Here, we offer ready-to-use production ready screens and not components that can be used to build the web and mobile apps.

Who can benefit the most from NativeBase Startup+?

Anyone who wants to convert their ideas into products. We offer a wide variety of screens so that users can use them directly in their apps. NativeBase Startup+ can save you on your production cost as well as the development time of your apps & website.

Does NativeBase Startup+ include Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD files?

The bundle includes Adobe XD source file only. We don't support Figma & Sketch file at the moment.
